Message from the Campus Director

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines Biñan Campus is pleased to announce its participation in the institutional accreditation process as part of our ongoing commitment to academic excellence and quality education. This comprehensive evaluation enables us to demonstrate our dedication to maintaining strong educational standards and ensuring that our programs effectively serve our students and the broader community. Through this process, we continue to focus on providing meaningful learning experiences, supporting student success, and maintaining the integrity and transparency that guide our institutional practices.The accreditation serves as both an opportunity to showcase our achievements and a valuable framework for identifying areas where we can further enhance our academic offerings, facilities, and support services. We approach this evaluation as a natural part of our institutional growth, recognizing it as an important step in ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for their careers and that our programs remain responsive to evolving industry and societal needs. We welcome, then, the opportunity to work and engage with the accreditation team in this comprehensive evaluation.We are grateful for the support of the PUP Office of the President, the Office of the Vice President for Campuses, the local government of the City of Biñan, the Quality Assurance Center, and all those who continue to guide and inspire us. With their support and the collective efforts of our dedicated campus community, we remain well-prepared and committed to this important endeavor, upholding the principles of accessible, quality higher education that have long defined our institution's mission and service to the community. Let this accreditation process reflect our continuous efforts to strengthen PUP Biñan as a center of learning, innovation, and public service.Maraming salamat po for joining us in this journey.
