Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE)

The BS Computer Engineering program bridges computing and electronics, training students to design and integrate hardware and software solutions for intelligent systems.

Highlights

  • Solid grounding in digital electronics, microprocessors, and embedded systems.
  • Robotics, automation, and IoT projects that apply theoretical concepts to real-world challenges.
  • Focus on systems integration, testing, and optimization for reliable computing devices.

Career Pathways

Graduates may pursue careers as computer engineers, embedded systems developers, hardware designers, test engineers, or technical specialists in manufacturing and technology firms.
