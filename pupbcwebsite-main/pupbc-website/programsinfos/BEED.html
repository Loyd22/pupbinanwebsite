Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd)

The Bachelor of Elementary Education program prepares future elementary teachers who can deliver inclusive, child-centered instruction across learning areas.

Highlights

  • Strong foundation in pedagogy, assessment, and differentiated instruction for young learners.
  • Integration of literacy, numeracy, science, and social studies teaching strategies.
  • Field study and practice teaching that strengthen classroom management and community engagement.

Career Pathways

Graduates can become licensed elementary teachers, learning facilitators, curriculum specialists, or education program coordinators in public and private schools.
